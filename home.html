Gorilla Realism

Gorilla Realism offers a fun game with semi-realistic graphics and physics. The maps include: Nextbots, KOTH, Mall, Forest, and Caves.

Why Play Gorilla Realism?

Experience Gorilla Tag like never before with enhanced physics, realistic movement, and unique maps to explore. Challenge yourself in KOTH, escape the Nextbots, or explore the Mall, Forest, and Caves!

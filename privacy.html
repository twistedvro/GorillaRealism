Gorilla Realism Privacy Policy

Gorilla Realism is developed by vrocacho

What data do we collect? How do we store your data?

Your login username and in-game settings are collected and stored by the Gorilla Realism game application directly on your headset. Passwords are never stored anywhere; instead a challenge is generated from your password that is then stored on our servers and used to authenticate your account. That digest is not personally identifiable.

The game application does not collect any analytics.

Any and all data on the headset can be deleted by deleting the application from the headset.

Any and all data on our servers is deleted automatically after 6 months of your last login.

If you wish to have your server data deleted before the default 6 months, contact us at vro@gorillarealism.com and we will confirm your account, remove the data and return an email confirming the removal.

Marketing

We will not reach out to you for marketing purposes.

Security of your personal data

The security of your personal data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. Gorilla Realism does not leak any of your personal data.

Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information & visitor behavior information. When you play our game, information could be collected from you automatically through cookies or similar technology. For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, though some features may not function as a result.
